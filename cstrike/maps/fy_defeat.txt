fy_defeat (Fight Yard)

by Tatu Eugen (www.tatueugen.info)

A simple map, based on 4 buildings in middle of the map,
4 tower in every corners, 2 for every team. 2 Walls after every base,
connected with every tower. From buildings you can go up on them.
Map for 32 players, bug reports: contact@tatueugen.info

Have fun!
