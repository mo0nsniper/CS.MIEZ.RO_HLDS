CSS_MIRAGE (Bomb/Defuse)

Map created by Tatu Eugen - www.tatueugen.info

This map is a remake from de_mirage of CS:S & CS:GO games,
map is recreated with all buildings, and some stuff from css/go,
and keep some things from original de_mirage of CS: 1.6

Any ideas and bugs please report at mail adress:
contact@tatueugen.info

Counter-Terrorists: Prevent Terrorists
from bombing explosive crates.
Team members must defuse any bombs 
that threaten targeted areas.

Terrorists: The Terrorist carrying the
C4 must destroy one of the explosive 
crate areas.

Other Notes: There are 2 bomb targets
in the mission.
