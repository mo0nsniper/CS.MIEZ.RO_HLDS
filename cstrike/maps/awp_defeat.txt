awp_defeat (Sniper War)

by Tatu Eugen (www.tatueugen.info)

A large map for sniper war, with an subteran area,
who have 2 ways, left and right, every base have an entry underground.
In middle of the map is an old building, can use for kill enemies, 
you can go up on the building, all players start with awp and deagle.
Map for 32 players, bug reports: contact@tatueugen.info

Have fun!
