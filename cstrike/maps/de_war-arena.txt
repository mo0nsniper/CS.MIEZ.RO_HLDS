War-Arena - Bomb/Defuse

by Tatu Eugen (www.tatueugen.info)

An city is evacuated after a terrorist escape from jail,
and attacked the city with his friends, 
after attack they destroy the bank and the bank car, but they are back
to steal the safe-deposit with all money inside from bank. 
A swat team is called by government to save the safe-deposit. 
Be careful the terrorist team want to destroy the police station also.

Counter-Terrorists: Prevent the terrorists
from destroying the bank or police station.

Terrorists: Destroy the bank or police station.

Other Notes: There are 2 bomb sites in this 
mission.
