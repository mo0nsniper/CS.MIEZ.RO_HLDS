map made for the ZTK mapping contest

A so called peaceful terrorist group have 
taken over a small outpost. Upon realizing 
that there encampment has been discovered 
they have decided to blow up there ammo dumps. 
The Counter Terrorist must beat them to 
the dumps in order to uncover the evidence 
and prove to the U.N that they are indeed 
Terrorists.
