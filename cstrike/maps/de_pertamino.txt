Pertamino - Bomb/Defuse
by SleekNut

One day, an oil company decided to raise the price of gasoline and LPG. All of people angry with that. To make even worse, a terrorist group decided to blow up one of the gas station and a truck carrying LPG.

Counter-Terrorists: Prevent Terrorists
from bombing the gas station & the truck. Team members must defuse any bombs that threaten targeted areas.

Terrorists: The Terrorist carrying the
C4 must destroy the gas station or the truck.

Other Notes: There are 2 bomb targets
in the mission.
